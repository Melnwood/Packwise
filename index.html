Setup & deployment

Demo mode works instantly with sample data saved in your browser. Live mode reads & writes your own Airtable base through a Netlify Function, so your token never touches the browser.

Move between your devices

No account needed. Export a backup file (it includes your trip, closet photos, list, and outfits), drop it in iCloud Drive or AirDrop it, then import it on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

Your private space

Everyone who uses Packwise gets their own space, so people only see their own clothes. This is your private access code — save it to open your closet on another device. Keep it private: anyone with this code can see your things.

Use a different code (open your space on another device, or start fresh)

To share Packwise with someone, just send them your site link — they open it, get their own code, and their closet stays separate from yours.

Auto-save across your devices (Airtable)

Your Packwise base is already created. Turn this on and the app saves everything automatically on every change — no Export button to remember — and loads it on your other devices too.

One-time setup (~3 minutes, on your Mac)

1. Create an Airtable personal access token at airtable.com/create/tokens with scopes data.records:read + data.records:write, given access to the Packwise base.
2. In Netlify → your site → Site settings → Environment variables, add AIRTABLE_PAT = your token, then redeploy.
3. Back here the Base ID is already filled — tap Save & connect, then flip the top toggle to Live. From then on it auto-connects and auto-saves.
